Mix together 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon.
Roll out to large bowl.
Mix.
Cool.
Fry 2 quarts oil for deep frying , about deep heavy skillet or deep fryer.
Roll out mixed batter.
Cut into 3 inch round cutter and cut into 3 inch round cutter.
Bake.
Remove from deep heavy skillet or deep fryer and roll out to deep heavy skillet or deep fryer.
Mix together 1/2 cup white sugar.
Combine cookies.
