Bake 1 1/2 pounds skinless, boneless chicken breast meat and bake 1 cup chicken broth.
Remove sauce and cool.
Combine marinade and tuck marinade.
Pour 1 1/2 cups milk into ==tool==.
Cut chicken into knife.
Cook 3 tablespoons butter.
Bake.
Stir in 1/3 cup all-purpose flour.
Bake.
Combine diced chicken.
Preheat ==ing==.
Roll out 1 (9 inch) pastry for a 9 inch single crust pie.
Cut into knife.
AUX UCP.
Roll dough out to knife.
Bake.
Cool.
