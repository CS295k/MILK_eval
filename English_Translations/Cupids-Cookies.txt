Beat 1 cup white sugar.
Stir in 1 egg.
Stir in 2 1/2 cups sifted all-purpose flour and stir in 1/2 teaspoon salt.
Stir in dough.
Top with dough and stir in dough.
Stir in batter.
Cool.
Preheat ==ing==.
Finished 2 tablespoons confectioners' sugar and roll out 2 tablespoons confectioners' sugar.
Cut into 2 1/2 and 1 1/2 inch heart shaped cookie cutters.
Place many 4-inch circles of dough in cookie sheets and bake.
Top with many 4-inch circles of dough and stir in many 4-inch circles of dough.
Stir in many 4-inch circles of dough.
Cool.
