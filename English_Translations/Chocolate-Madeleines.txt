Preheat ==ing==.
Preheat 1/4 cup sifted all-purpose flour and add 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder.
Add 2 ounces butter and add 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract.
Place 1 egg, 2 egg yolks, 2 ounces butter, 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract, 1/4 cup white sugar in Madeleine pan and bake .
Remove 1 egg, 2 egg yolks, 2 ounces butter, 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract, 1/4 cup white sugar, 1/4 cup sifted all-purpose flour, 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder, 1/2 teaspoon baking powder, 1 pinch salt and cool.
