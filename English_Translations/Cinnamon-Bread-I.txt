Preheat ==ing== and flour ==ing==.
Mix together 2 eggs.
Pour mixed chicken into 9x5 inch loaf pan and roll mixed chicken out to 9x5 inch loaf pan.
Mix together 2 teaspoons margarine and bake 2 tablespoons white sugar.
Combine sauce.
Cut into knife and inserted into knife.
Bake.
Roll cookies out to toothpick.
Pour cookies into toothpick and inserted near toothpick.
Remove from toothpick and pour cookies into toothpick.
Cool.
