Preheat ==ing==.
Roll out 6 center cut pork chops, trimmed of any visible fat and stir together 6 center cut pork chops, trimmed of any visible fat.
Coated with 1/4 cup egg substitute.
Stir in 1/4 cup all-purpose flour and cook 2 cups Italian-style seasoned bread crumbs.
Place batter on large skillet.
Bake.
Place cookies in 9x13 inch baking dish and roll cookies out to 9x13 inch baking dish.
Bake.
Place 1 (10.75 ounce) can  reduced fat condensed cream of mushroom soup in medium bowl.
Pour baked cookies into ==tool==.
Roll dough out to ==tool==.
Bake.
