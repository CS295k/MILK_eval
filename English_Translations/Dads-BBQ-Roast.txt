Mix together 1 1/3 cups yellow mustard.
Place 1 (5 pound) beef rump roast in 2 long sheets of aluminum foil and add 1 (5 pound) beef rump roast.
Roll out to 2 long sheets of aluminum foil.
Preheat rolled dough and bake.
Bake and adjust rolled dough.
Refrigerate.
Place rolled dough in deep serving plate and cut into deep serving plate.
