Pull 2 tablespoons olive oil, divided and cook 2 tablespoons olive oil, divided.
Place 2 (6 ounce) skinless, boneless chicken breast halves in skillet.
Mix.
Bake and bake.
Remove from skillet.
Fold in paper towels and cut into paper towels.
Mix together 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar.
Mix.
Serve.
