Preheat ==ing==.
Flute 4 pounds boneless chuck roast and scatter 4 pounds boneless chuck roast.
Add 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce and add 1 (1 ounce) package dry onion and mushroom soup mix.
Add 2 carrots, chopped and combine 2 stalks celery, chopped.
Flute vegetables and stir in vegetables.
Flute dough and place dough in roasting pan.
Preheat rolled dough and simmer.
