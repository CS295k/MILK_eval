Place butter in medium saucepan and stir together butter.
Bake.
Remove from medium saucepan and place flour in medium saucepan.
Place 1/4 teaspoon salt on medium saucepan and bake.
Remove from medium saucepan.
Mix in medium saucepan.
Roll dough out to spoon or pastry bag fitted with No. 10 or larger tip.
Place rolled dough in cookie sheet and bake.
Preheat rolled dough and inserted near oven.
Cool.
Combine package and stir together package.
Combine heavy cream and bake heavy cream.
Mix.
Pour tasty whipped cream into medium bowl and stir in tasty whipped cream.
Roll out rolled dough.
Stir in dough.

Pour into medium saucepan and stir in chocolate.
Bake.

Bake.
Remove dough and cool.
