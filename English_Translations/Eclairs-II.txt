Preheat ==ing== and cover ==ing==.
Place 1/2 cup butter in medium saucepan and stir together 1/2 cup butter.
Bake.
Remove from medium saucepan and place 1 cup all-purpose flour in medium saucepan.
Place 1/4 teaspoon salt on medium saucepan and bake.
Remove from medium saucepan.
Mix in medium saucepan.
Roll dough out to spoon or pastry bag fitted with No. 10 or larger tip.
Place rolled dough in cookie sheet and bake.
Preheat rolled dough and inserted near oven.
Cool.
Combine 1 (5 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix and stir together 1 (5 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix.
Combine 1 cup heavy cream and bake 1 cup heavy cream.
Mix.
Pour blended batter into medium bowl and stir in blended batter.
Roll out rolled dough.
Stir in dough.
Remove ==ing==.
Pour into medium saucepan and stir in 2 (1 ounce) squares semisweet chocolate.
Bake.
Pour 1 cup confectioners' sugar into medium saucepan and stir in 1 cup confectioners' sugar.
Bake.
Remove dough and cool.
Stir in sauced circles.
Cool.
