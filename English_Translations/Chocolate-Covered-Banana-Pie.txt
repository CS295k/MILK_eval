Spread 3 cups crushed vanilla wafers on 9 x 13 inch pan.
Press batter into 9 x 13 inch pan.
Bake.
Cool and divide in ==tool==.
Top with 4 1/2 cups miniature marshmallows and drop in sauce pan.
Stir in cookies.
Cool.
Stir in 2 large bananas, sliced.
Roll out 2 (3.5 ounce) packages instant vanilla pudding mix and combine 2 (3.5 ounce) packages instant vanilla pudding mix.
Combine dough.
Combine batter.
Combine 1 (1.5 ounce) bar milk chocolate candy.
Cool and serve.
