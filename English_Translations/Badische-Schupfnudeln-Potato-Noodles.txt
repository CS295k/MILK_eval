Cook and place 1 1/2 pounds russet potatoes in large pot.
Roll out 1 1/2 pounds russet potatoes and place 1 1/2 pounds russet potatoes in rolling pin.
Add 1 1/2 pounds russet potatoes and mix together 1/2 teaspoon salt.
Roll out mixed ingredients.
Cut mixed ingredients into knife.
Roll out mixed ingredients and season with mixed ingredients.
